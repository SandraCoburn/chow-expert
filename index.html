Dr. Clifton Shryock Jr. and Marlene Coburn

Some of Clif's passion were judging Dog Shows and Chow dogs breeding. On February 14th, 1987 Clif married Marlene Coburn. They made their home in Newport Beach, CA.

Clif - The Judge

Clif was the "great gentleman" of the Chow world.

For so many years Clif traveled extensively while he served as a judge at national and international Chow breed dog competition shows. He was frequently featured in Dog's magazines for his involvement and contribution to the success of these shows.A respected Judge in Conformation, Obedience and Junior Handling for more than just Chows.

Clif - Chow Breeder and Author

Clif was the "great gentleman" of the Chow world.

He was known most for importing Ghat and establishing the bloodlines for which many of the chows of today came from, de la Moulaine Kennels are the foundation to so many bloodlines. He was also author to "How To Raise and Train A Chow Chow." Ghat is on the cover of this wonderful little book.
